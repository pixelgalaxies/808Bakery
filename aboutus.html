About Us:

Our Journey...

In 1986, 808 Bakery opened its first location on a quiet street corner in the heart of Honolulu. From the beginning 808 Bakery has been cherished for its classic American baked goods, and warm, inviting atmosphere. We strive to make the best pastries on the island, keeping our regular customers as well as bringing in new ones. We are guided by the notion that charity begins at home and then extends to the global community, from daily donations to relief efforts we are committed to supporting the community. In the past we have partnered with breast cancer organizations and school bake sales.

Mission Statement

Serving tiramisu, crème brulée, and macarons made fresh daily, 808 Bakery's goal is to make and serve the finest special delicacies at an affordable price, to make every day feel like a special occasion, or to make your special occasion a truly memorable one. Our items are made from scratch from simple ingredients in our bakery. We use no preservatives or fillers. 808 Bakery is locally owned and operated.
